# What's New in One Delhi v1.0.2 🚀

* 🔄 **Quick Station Swap**: Added a handy reverse/swap button next to the From-To inputs so you can instantly switch your boarding and destination stops with a single tap.
* 🕒 **Recent Journeys History**: The app now displays a quick-list of your most recent successful bookings, allowing you to instantly reload your favorite route details with 1-tap.
* 💓 **Tactile Haptic Feedback**: Enjoy premium physical vibration feedback when performing actions like swapping stations or validating details.
* ⚠️ **Inline Input Highlights**: Replaced old system alert popups with modern, clean red border indicators for missing input fields.
* ⏱️ **Fast & Lightweight**: Configured optimized CPU-specific downloads (`arm64-v8a`, `armeabi-v7a`, `x86_64`) that reduce the app download size by over 60%!
* 💸 **Clearer Payment Formatting**: Price displays during ticket purchases now show exact pricing (with decimals) across Paytm, PhonePe, and UPI screens.
* 💳 **Polished Payment Experience**: Refactored transition flows and checkmark success animations for Paytm and PhonePe payments.
* 🔋 **Battery Optimization**: Improved background tasks to significantly reduce battery usage while active.

Enjoy your ride with **One Delhi**! Your feedback helps us improve.
